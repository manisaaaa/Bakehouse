About Us

Little Abrm Bakes is a home-based bakery business that provides fresh and delicious mini baked items such as mini cakes, donuts, cupcakes, cookies, brownies and custom sweet treats. These treats are perfect for birthdays, small celebrations, gifts and personal cravings.

Vision
